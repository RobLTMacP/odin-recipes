Gyudon!

Fresh Gyudon:

Wonderfully sweet and savory beef and rice dish originating from Japan.

Ingredients:

  • Shaved beef
  • White rice
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • White onion
  • Sesame seeds
  • Dashi powder

Steps:

  1. Prepare dashi broth
  2. Simmer onions
  3. Add sake, mirin and season
  4. Add in the beef and cook until tender
  5. Seperately prepare the rice
  6. Add beef and seasoned broth over the rice
  7. Finish with sesame seeds or pickled vegetables
  8. Enjoy!
